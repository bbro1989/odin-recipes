Cookie Dough Cupcakes Recipe

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish known for its delicious combination of a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and a variety of toppings.
Its versatility allows for endless creativity, making it a favorite meal around the world for both casual gatherings and gourmet experiences.

A classic pizza typically features a thin or thick dough base, spread with tangy tomato sauce and topped with mozzarella cheese.
From pepperoni and mushrooms to olives and bell peppers, the choice of toppings can be tailored to suit any taste preference, making each pizza a unique culinary creation.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra olive oil for drizzling

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Mix and knead until a smooth dough forms.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  6. Transfer the dough to a baking tray or pizza stone.
  7. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add fresh basil leaves.
  9. Drizzle with a little olive oil for extra flavor.
  10. Bake in the oven for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  11. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
